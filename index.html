About Us

Ruchika Furnishings, led by Gokul Prasad, is a family-owned carpentry business with over 30 years of experience.
Our craft is more than just work—it's a legacy passed down from my grandfather to my father, and then to me.
This tradition of skill and dedication inspires every piece we create.
From custom furniture to home renovations and storage solutions, we blend inherited talent with modern techniques to deliver woodwork that's functional, beautiful, and built to last.
